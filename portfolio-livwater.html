The Brief

Category-defining technology.
Brand built to match.

LIVWater is building next-generation decentralised water infrastructure — a Water-as-a-Service model that transfers risk from communities and governments to private capital. Their technology and operating model were ahead of the market. Their brand and business architecture were not.

Archetype Studio was engaged to build the full commercial system: a brand narrative that could speak to institutional investors and enterprise partners simultaneously, a structured go-to-market framework for asset-light geographic expansion, and the marketing infrastructure to execute at scale.

The result is a brand that carries the weight of infrastructure — precise, considered, and built to endure.

Sector Water Infrastructure · WaaS
Scope Brand + Business + Marketing
Markets Global · Sub-Saharan Africa
Audience Institutional Investors · Partners
What We Built

Brand. Business. Infrastructure.

01 — Brand Strategy

WaaS Category Narrative

Category-defining positioning for the Water-as-a-Service model. Investor and enterprise partner messaging, narrative architecture, and a brand story built for institutional credibility — across capital raise and enterprise sales cycles.

02 — Identity

Visual Identity System

Full brand system: logo, typography, colour, motion principles, and imagery direction. Designed for infrastructure scale — from pitch decks and investor materials to field operations and digital product.

03 — Architecture

Business Architecture

Revenue model structuring, partnership frameworks, and a go-to-market blueprint for asset-light expansion into new geographies without operational chaos at the centre.

04 — Marketing

Marketing Infrastructure

Full-funnel content strategy, investor communication suite, digital presence, and campaign infrastructure for enterprise lead generation and community engagement across target markets.
